<Update label="6 March 2026">

## In-app support requests

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/in-app-support.png" alt="Support option in the user menu" />
</Frame>

You can now contact our support team directly from the application. Click your name in the top-right corner and select **Support** to open the support form. Describe your issue and submit. The system automatically captures a screenshot of your current page and collects diagnostic information (such as browser details and any errors you may have encountered) so our team can understand and resolve your issue faster. You can expect a response within two working days.

## Standardised registration numbers

All premises registration numbers have been updated to a consistent 10-digit format. Existing registration numbers have been automatically converted, so no action is needed on your part. This makes registration numbers easier to reference and share.

Learn more about [premises registration numbers](/premises/registration-numbers).

<Update label="February 2025">

## Two-factor authentication

You can now enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your ePass account for added security. When enabled, you will need to enter a code from your authenticator app each time you sign in. Recovery codes are provided in case you lose access to your device.

Learn more about [setting up 2FA](/authentication/two-factor-authentication).

</Update>
