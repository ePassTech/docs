title: "Features"
description: "An overview of what you can do with ePass"
---

## Licence applications

Apply for licences through a guided, step-by-step process. Answer register-specific questions, review your details at each stage, and submit with a legal declaration. Track your application from submission through to approval, and pick up where you left off at any time.

## Payments

Pay licence fees securely through Gov.uk Pay. If a payment fails, you can retry without resubmitting your application. Staff users can view transaction history and process refunds.

## Premises management

Register and manage premises linked to your organisation. ePass supports fixed, mobile, online, and temporary premises types. Saved premises can be reused across multiple applications, and staff users can search, filter, and export premises data.

## Organisation management

Set up and manage organisations as a company, partnership, sole trader, local council, or third sector body. Link registered companies directly from Companies House with auto-populated address data, and manage multiple organisations each with their own licences.

## E-learning

Complete interactive learning modules with quizzes and progress tracking. Your progress is saved so you can return and continue at any time. Some modules are available publicly without signing in.

## Self-assessment

After your application is approved, complete a short self-assessment for each premises. These include video content, educational material, and questions that must be answered correctly before you can proceed.

## Bookings and inspections

Staff users can create and manage inspection bookings, assign inspectors, and schedule visits. Inspections follow structured checklists that are completed and reviewed before submission. Businesses are notified when an announced inspection is scheduled.

## Change requests

Request amendments to approved registrations, such as updating details, changing status, or adding and removing premises. Requests follow a draft-and-submit workflow with approval by a team admin. Rejected requests include a reason and can be reopened.

## Certificates and documents

Download your licence certificate as a PDF once your application is approved. Staff users can also generate and download full application summaries.

## Notifications

Receive email and SMS notifications for key events including application approvals, scheduled inspections, change request updates, and data exports.

## Public register

Anyone can search the public register to look up registered premises without signing in. The register supports filtering by local authority, business type, status, and more.

## Dashboard

Get a summary view when you sign in. Citizens see their registrations, in-progress applications, and available registers. Staff users see application trends, premises totals, and change request activity.

## Audit and compliance

All data access, changes, and exports are logged with a full audit trail. Activity history is visible to staff users on a per-application and per-premises basis.
