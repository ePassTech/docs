title: "Login"
description: "Sign in to your ePass account"
---

ePass is accessed through your web browser. To sign in, navigate to your organisation's ePass application URL (e.g., `app.epass.service.gov.scot`).

<Info>
  **Prerequisite**: You need an ePass account to sign in. Registration is by invitation only. You will receive a signed invitation link from your organisation administrator. You cannot self-register.
</Info>

## Signing in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Open your browser and navigate to the ePass application URL provided by your organisation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your **email address** and **password** in the login form.

    Optionally, tick the **Remember me** checkbox to stay signed in on your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete two-factor authentication (if enabled)">
    If your account has two-factor authentication enabled, you will be redirected to a verification page after entering your password. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the challenge.

    For more details, see [Two-factor authentication](/authentication/two-factor-authentication).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    After successful authentication, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resetting your password

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it from the login page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot your password?'">
    On the login page, click the **Forgot your password?** link below the password field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Enter the email address associated with your ePass account and submit the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    You will receive an email containing a password reset link. Click the link to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Enter your new password and confirm it, then submit the form. You can now sign in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I am locked out after too many login attempts">
    For security, ePass limits login attempts to **5 per minute** per email and IP address combination. If you exceed this limit, you will need to wait before trying again. Ensure you are entering the correct credentials before retrying.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I did not receive a password reset email">
    If you do not receive the password reset email:

    1. Check your spam or junk folder.
    2. Verify that you entered the correct email address.
    3. Wait a few minutes. Email delivery can sometimes be delayed.
    4. If the email still does not arrive, contact your organisation administrator or [ePass support](mailto:support@epass.tech).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I do not have an ePass account">
    ePass accounts are created by invitation only. Contact your organisation administrator to request an invitation link. You cannot create an account yourself.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
