## Two-factor authentication

You can now enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your ePass account for added security. When enabled, you will need to enter a code from your authenticator app each time you sign in. Recovery codes are provided in case you lose access to your device.

Learn more about [setting up 2FA](/authentication/two-factor-authentication).
