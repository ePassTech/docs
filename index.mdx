## What is ePass

ePass is a digital licensing platform operated on behalf of the Scottish Government. It manages licensing for tobacco retail, funeral directors, and food business registration across Scotland.

## Applications

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="building-columns" title="app.epass" href="https://app.epass.service.gov.scot">
    **Admin Portal** for Scottish Government licensing and enforcement teams. Process and manage licence applications, inspections, and compliance.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="user" title="my.epass" href="https://my.epass.service.gov.scot">
    **Applicant Hub** for citizens and business owners. Apply for licences, manage premises, and track applications all in one place.
